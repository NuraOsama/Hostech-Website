Reception Program

The main objectives to be applied in this program:

  • Opening new patient files and recording the patient's personal data.
  • Searching the data of registered patients by all available methods with this data recall.
  • Use the personal fingerprint registration mechanisms to identify the owner of the service with the file.
  • Using the mechanism of extracting magnetic cards or barcode cards with patient data.
  • Opening a new visit for the patient and adding the services of the different departments in it.
  • Follow-up of the transfers sent from each hospital division, the ability to print reports.
  • The ability to add, modify and delete services transferred from clinics according to the powers.
  • Receiving departmental reports with the ability to print them.
  • A detailed record of the data and files of the patients and the employees and their dependents in charge of the medical service.
  • The ability to search on patient visits and know the services provided in each visit.
  • The ability to follow the movements of a specific patient between departments.
  • Each employee receives his own account with his personal name and password.
  • The ability to cancel an order (a set of services) by the system administrator.
  • The ability to print the receipt or transfer document more than once without having to register it again.
  • All available jobs and capabilities are subject to the powers set by the system administrator.

Archive Patient Files:

  • The patient's past data has been completely saved and fully archived in his medical file.
  • Send the files expired within the clinic archive data.
  • Recalling the patient's old electronic files from the archive with the ability to amend them.
  • The possibility of opening additional files for the same patient.
  • Searching archives of files in several ways.

Reception dealt with departments, services, and system settings:

  • The Ability to add new departments and services.
  • The Ability to modify and delete services and departments.
  • The Identification of hospital personnel through the database.
  • Direct connection to the laboratory, radiology, first aid and other departments.

Reception Reports and Statistics:

  • Receiving and printing reports and results of laboratories, laboratories and x-rays.
  • Receive and print registered medical reports from clinics.
  • Report and transfers of the receptionist during a specific period.
  • The Report of the employee or student and his subordinates.
  • A detailed report of transfers for a specific employee or group of employees daily or during a specific period.
  • A detailed patient visits report and the services provided to them by the visit.
  • A collection of statistical reports combined with other sections in more than one way of presentation.
