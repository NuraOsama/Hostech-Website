Radiology Department Program

The main objectives to be applied in this program:

  • Preparing all types of radiation, recording and preparing their reports.
  • The ability to link x-ray images from devices to the system database automatically I have to serve the radiology devices dealing with the technologies used.
  • Receive reports and pictures of the rays from the units, print them on the date of their delivery, or send them to the clinic.
  • Large collection of statistical reports on Preparing the patients, the attendees, the results and the comparisons.
  • Receiving radiology requests in accordance with the transfers, receiving transfers and clinics.
  • Send results and x-rays automatically to the relevant clinic.
  • The ability to print reports from the units directly or print them from reception.
  • The ability to record the names of specialists and technicians on the results, with terms of reference defined for each unit From radiology units.
  • The ability to search for specific results or x-rays of a certain type, in a specific clinic or patient.
  • Possibility of linking and integrating with PACS systems.
